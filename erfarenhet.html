Internship VR Developer, Vobling

December 2021 — May 2023

A significant contribution I made was to revamp their scenario loading system. Initially, JSON was used, but I introduced the use of scriptable objects to enhance the loading process. Additionally, I developed a comprehensive tutorial to guide users in effectively utilizing the application. These valuable improvements and additions are present in their latest app update. Throughout the internship, I deepened my understanding of C# .net, agile methodologies, and the effective use of Git as a version control system. This experience has contributed to my ability to work smoothly and collaboratively within development teams.
